Our Story

Oceanside's Locally Owned Cleaning Company

Oceanside Cleaning Service was founded right here in Oceanside, CA, with a simple mission: to provide the most thorough, reliable, and professional house cleaning service in North San Diego County. We're not a franchise or a national chain — we're your neighbors, and we take that seriously.

Over the years, we've built a loyal base of customers across Oceanside's neighborhoods — from the beachside homes near the pier to the family communities in Ivey Ranch, Fire Mountain, San Luis Rey, and Ocean Hills. Our reputation is built entirely on word-of-mouth referrals and our consistent 5-star Google ratings.

Every member of our cleaning team is carefully vetted through background checks and trained to meet our exacting standards. Whether you're booking a regular weekly cleaning or a one-time deep cleaning service, you can expect the same level of professionalism and attention to detail every single visit.
